## Purpose

The Clinical Intelligence Agent is an AI-powered assistant that answers questions about medical guidelines and insurance coverage using official NICE documents and predefined insurance plan rules. The system provides grounded responses based on retrieved content and supports safe decision-making through clarification and human review.

## Intended Users

- End users seeking information about medical guidelines and insurance coverage

- Insurance support staff

- Internal reviewers

- System administrators

## Core Capabilities

- Answers natural language questions using retrieved guideline documents

- Applies insurance plan rules (Silver / Gold / Bronze)

- Requests clarification for ambiguous queries

- Escalates uncertain or high-risk queries for human review

- Logs all interactions for audit and quality assurance

## Out of Scope

- Medical diagnosis

- Emergency medical advice

- Personalized treatment recommendations

- Clinical decision-making without human oversight
